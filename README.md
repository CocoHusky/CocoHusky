## About Me

I am a biomedical engineer from the University of Arizona focused on implantable and wearable medical systems, miniaturized sensing devices, and full-stack biomedical product development. I like building across electronics, housings, embedded firmware, data pipelines, applications, and validation so complex sensing ideas become simple, usable systems.
