About Me

I am a biomedical engineer from the University of Arizona focused on implantable and wearable medical systems, miniature device design, and the full stack of biomedical product development. I care about building tools that help people collect better data about themselves and use that data to improve their lives.

I enjoy hands-on work across electronics, mechanical housings, embedded programming, applications, data pipelines, and validation. My strength is taking complex sensing and medical-device problems and reducing them into simple, buildable systems.
